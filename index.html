Steps to Create Your Own Artwork:

  1. Choose your medium (paint, digital, photography)
  2. Sketch or plan your composition
  3. Add details and refine your work
  4. Sign your artwork
  5. Display it proudly

Common Materials Used:

  • Canvas or paper
  • Paints: oil, acrylic, watercolor
  • Brushes of various sizes
  • Digital tablet and stylus
  • Previous versions or sketches (discarded ideas)
